What data is deleted?

When you request deletion, we permanently remove:

  • Your Bantu Stream Connect account
  • Name & email
  • Profile photo
  • Uploaded content (videos, audio, thumbnails)*
  • Likes, comments & saved items
  • App usage analytics (Supabase buckets)
  • Authentication tokens
  • Search history

*Important: Creator content may have up to a 30-day retention period for copyright, fraud, or legal compliance purposes.

Children's Privacy (Families Policy)

Since Bantu Stream Connect may be used by younger audiences, we follow the Google Play Families Policy, including:

Data Protection for Children

  • No personalized advertising to children
  • Highest standards of data minimization
  • Parental consent requirements for data collection

Extra Safeguards

  • Extra verification steps for deletion requests
  • Special procedures for minors' guardians
  • Enhanced privacy settings for young users

Note: If you are submitting a deletion request on behalf of a minor, please include "Minor Account Deletion" in your email subject and provide proof of guardianship.
