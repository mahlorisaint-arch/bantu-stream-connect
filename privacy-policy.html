Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways:

(A) Information You Provide Directly

Account Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Encrypted password
  • Date of birth (used for age verification)

Payment Information

If you subscribe, rent, purchase, or tip creators:

  • Transaction details
  • Payment confirmation data

(We do not store your full credit/debit card number. Payments are handled by secure third-party processors such as Stripe, Paystack, or mobile money partners.)

Creator Payout Information

For creators receiving revenue:

  • Bank account or mobile money details
  • Tax documents (if legally required)

Support Information

  • Emails, support tickets, and communication logs

(B) Information Collected Automatically (Usage Data)

Device & Connection Information

  • Device type and model
  • Device ID
  • Operating system and version
  • Browser type
  • IP address
  • Mobile network provider
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

Usage & Interaction Data

  • Videos watched
  • Time spent watching
  • Search queries
  • Playback actions (pause, rewind, skip)
  • Likes, comments, shares, follows
  • Viewing patterns and recommendations clicked
  • Real-time performance data (buffering, bitrate, quality)

General Location Information

  • Region/country inferred from IP
  • Only collects precise GPS location if you explicitly opt in

(C) Information From Third Parties

  • Payment processors (to confirm transactions)
  • Authentication providers (if you sign in using Google, Apple, etc.)
  • Advertising partners (for ad-supported content tiers)
  • Analytics services (Firebase, Google Analytics)

Data Storage & Security

A. Where We Store Data

Your information is stored on secure cloud servers located in:

  • South Africa
  • And/or GDPR-compliant international data centers

We use distributed infrastructure to ensure uptime, performance, and resilience.

B. How We Protect Your Data

We use industry-standard security controls, including:

  • Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest (AES-256)
  • Role-based access controls (RBAC)
  • Multi-factor authentication for internal systems
  • Routine security audits and penetration tests
5

When We Share Your Information

We never sell your data.

We share information only under the following circumstances:

A. With Creators (Limited & Anonymized Data Only)

Creators may receive:

  • Total view counts
  • Engagement statistics
  • Anonymous demographic summaries (e.g., "viewers from South Africa")

We do not share:

  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Password
  • Private account details

B. With Trusted Third-Party Service Providers

Including:

  • Payment processors
  • Analytics tools (e.g., Firebase)
  • Cloud storage & hosting providers
  • Advertising partners (for ad-supported streaming tiers)

Advertising partners may receive:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA / Ad ID)
  • Anonymous interaction metrics

You can opt out of targeted advertising in your device settings.

C. For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information if required by:

  • Subpoena
  • Court order
  • Government mandate
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users or platform
